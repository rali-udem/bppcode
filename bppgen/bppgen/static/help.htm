This is the user interface to BPPGen, a letter generator for headhunters. BPPGen relies on Gentext, an experimental multi-purpose Natural Language Generation framework. Gentext uses JsRealB for realization.

BPPGen and Gentext were developed by Philippe Grand'Maison for the ButterflyPredictiveProject (BPP), under the supervision of Professor Guy Lapalme. BPP is a joint research project between RALI (Université de Montréal) and LittleBIGJob.
